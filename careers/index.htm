EVO Business Solutions is comprised of individuals who believe in excellence. We specialize in hiring people who have the drive to succeed in their career and the will to implement the discipline required.

Working with us affords you an opportunity to work with some of the premier companies both in and out of Malaysia. Catering to their needs means that Evo has opportunities for both short term or long term positions.

See below for more information and some of our current roles.

What does EVO do?

When you work with us, you get to be part of a team driven to perform. Our focus is providing outsourcing solutions to our clients with above expectation delivery.

What industries do you specialize in?

EVO Business Consulting focuses not just on recruiting individuals that are performance oriented, but also on developing them. To us it is important to ensure that our clients are satisfied and are being served with the care and respect they deserve.

EVO and partners are involved in nearly every industry with a particular focus on:

  • Banking and Finance
  • Telco and Communications
  • Technology
  • Education
  • Retail
