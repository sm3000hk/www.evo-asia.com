about us

EVO Business Consulting Sdn Bhd is an outsourcing arm of PERSOLKELLY that offers complete outsourcing services to all our esteemed customers. We are in association with PERSOLKELLY Malaysia, the largest end-to-end HR solutions provider in Asia Pacific that covers temporary, contract & permanent staffing, payroll outsourcing, headhunting, and HR consulting services.

We provide Scope of Work (SOW) on Managed Services, partial and full-fledged Business Process Outsourcing Services including the Build Operate Transfer (BOT) model. Our services line-up such as Training Administrative Outsourcing, Program Management Outsourcing, Invoice and Document Verification and Validation, Total Payroll Outsourcing, Inbound & Outbound Call Center Outsourcing, Multi-linguistics Customer Service Support (including Thai, Viet, Indo, Urdu, Vietnam, Arabic, Pashto and etc) as well as  IT Helpdesk, Project Management, Background Check Verification(BGC) and Freelancer Management for our clients in Malaysia.

 

our approach

EVO approaches every client’s business as if it were our own. We put ourselves in our clients’ shoes and collaborate to unlock the full potential of their business. 

The right approach is necessary for the right outcome. EVO approaches work by applying its external knowledge to your organization’s internal way of doing work. We know that in order to maximize the potential of success for your company we need to shape our expert advice in a way that applies to your way of doing business. This allows us to create rich relationships with our clients.
